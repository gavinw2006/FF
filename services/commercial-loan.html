Commercial property finance requires a different approach to residential lending. Lenders assess rental income, business cashflow, and property type — all of which affect your borrowing capacity and rate.

Our brokers have deep experience in commercial lending and work with specialist lenders who understand income-producing properties and complex ownership structures.
