Overseas Income Lending

Buying in Australia with Foreign Income

Many Australian residents and citizens working abroad want to maintain a property foothold in Australia. Standard lenders often struggle to assess overseas income — but specialist lenders on our panel have clear policies designed for this exact scenario.

We guide you through the documentation requirements, currency conversion rules, and lender selection to maximise your chances of approval at the best possible rate.
