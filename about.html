About Further Finance Group

Our Story

Built on Trust Since 2005

Further Finance Group was founded with a clear vision: to build a team of credible, professional mortgage brokers that local communities could rely on for honest, expert financial guidance.

Over two decades, we have helped thousands of Australians achieve their property goals — from first-home buyers navigating the lending landscape for the first time, to experienced investors expanding their portfolios.

Our access to hundreds of finance products from more than 20 banks and private finance organisations means we can always find a solution that fits your situation, not the other way around.
