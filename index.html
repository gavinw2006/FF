How It Works

Our 7-Step Process

A clear, straightforward path from first contact to loan settlement.

1

Contact & Appointment

Reach out to book a free initial consultation with one of our brokers.

2

Assess Serviceability

We review your income, expenses, and financial position to determine your borrowing capacity.

3

Collect Documents

We guide you through gathering all documents required for your application.

4

Pre-Approval

We submit for conditional approval so you can search for property with confidence.

5

Contract Signed

Once you find your property and sign the contract, we proceed to formal application.

6

Unconditional Approval

The lender formally approves your loan — you're cleared to proceed to settlement.

7

Loan Settlement

Funds are released, property ownership transfered, and your loan begins. Your new property settled! Congratulations!
